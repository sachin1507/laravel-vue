5.5 

Cancellation of Commitment 

(a) 

The Facility A Commitments which, at that time, are unutilised shall be immediately cancelled at the 
end of the Availability Period for Facility A. 

(b) 

The Facility B Commitments which, at that time, are unutilised shall be immediately cancelled at the 
end of the Availability Period for Facility B. 

(c) 

The Facility C Commitments which, at that time, are unutilised shall be immediately cancelled at the 
end of the Availability Period for Facility C. 

6. 

SELECTION OF CURRENCY 

(a) 

The Company shall select the currency of a Loan in a Utilisation Request.

Each Loan will remain denominated for its next Interest Period in the same currency as that in which 
it is denominated, pursuant to paragraph (a) above, for its current Interest Period.
